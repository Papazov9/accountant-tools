How It Works

Forget about manually comparing long Excel sheets. With our solution, you can automate the comparison process in just a few steps.

Step 1: Upload Files

Upload your invoice files (CSV or TXT) for comparison.

Step 2: Compare Data

Our system matches records by key data points like document number, date, and amount.

Step 3: Review Results

Receive a detailed Excel report with separate sheets for each type of mismatch, giving you a clear breakdown of errors like date mismatches, document number inconsistencies, and more.
